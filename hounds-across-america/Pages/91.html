Fort Worth Herd star

We're here to see the Fort Worth Herd of longhorn cattle, driven down the street twice daily. The star is one of many in the area commemorating cowboy and cattle-industry legends. The dogs are wearing neckerchiefs from the Pendleton whiskey girls. The humans are enjoying the whiskey.
