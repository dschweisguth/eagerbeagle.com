Pets are not allowed in food establishments

Oregon is much less dog-friendly than California, and the boys had to put up with this sort of thing at about every other restaurant and winery on our trip. Here they exemplify the situation at the Klamath Basin Brewing Company's flaghship Creamery restaurant. We consoled them by throwing fries through the fence from our nearby table.
