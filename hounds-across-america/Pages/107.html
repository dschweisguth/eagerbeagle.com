King Felix III

King Felix is the white head of Mobile's Mardi Gras, which predates that in New Orleans. A statue of his more recently introduced black counterpart is on the other side of the plaza. Besides "Blues in the Night", I had been drawn to Mobile by the excellent documentary "The Order of Myths", about the color line in Mobile's Mardi Gras.
