Choctaw Hall

Our luckiest lodging find of the trip, an 1836 mansion whose gracious owners gave us a tour before we continued on. They have a distinguished old family's heritage of furniture, tableware and decor, and continue to improve the house and add to their collection. I hope to return.

Halley Beagle    Mississippi    Natchez    Wallace Beagle
