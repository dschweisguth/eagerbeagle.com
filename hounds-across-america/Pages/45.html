Sunset at Goulding's

I made a point of reaching Monument Valley before sunset. We stayed in a villa at Goulding's, a sprawling, long-standing business with rooms, villas, camping, a restaurant, a store and a gas station. Goulding's is just over the Utah border and in the Navajo nation, so the store doesn't sell alcohol, only Martinell's.
