Mission San Antonio de Padua




Up  Next-->

Huxley poses in the courtyard of our new favorite mission -- except for Mission Dolores, of course! Mission San Antonio is 20' from route 101 in the middle of Fort Hunter Liggett. The whole building is in active use, but it retains an air of untouched rurality and authenticity. As you can see, well-behaved dogs are allowed in the courtyard.
